Return of Sweatsuit Man
Yes, he’s back again.
This time, he has a vehicle. I saw him parked along the curb, just across the street
from my building. He was in this dirt-caked pickup truck with a crooked fender. Just
staring at the main entrance like it was a bank he was about to rob.
I guess that rules out homeless.
Who the hell is that guy? I don’t like it. And I’m really, really trying not to be
paranoid. Really.
